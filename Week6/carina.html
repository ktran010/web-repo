Places to do a Photoshoot in Denver

    Union Station

    • Union Station has to be one of the most photographable train stations there is. The interior and exterior of the building are both stunning. You can snap a picture in front of the iconic union station sign, or inside at the dainty flower shop. The options here are endless.

    Pastel Row on Colfax Ave

    • Pastel Row is a collection of townhomes all painted in vibrant, pastel colors. If you are looking to add some color to your Instagram feed, this is the place for you.

    Red Rocks Amphitheater

    • If you’ve ever stepped foot into Colorado, we know you’ve heard of Red Rocks. While it’s 10 miles west of Denver, we HAD to include this iconic spot. Whether its a sunrise photo or selfies from a concert, the photos here are breathtaking. You won’t regret checking out this spot!

    Denver Botanic Gardens

    • At the Denver Botanic Gardens, you can explore 24 acres of picture-perfect spots. There is a different photogenic bridge, plant, and gazebo around every corner. You HAVE to stop here if you want a new nature spot to switch up your scenery.

    RiNo Arts District

    • Rino Arts District is filled with vibrant, colorful art murals. There are a variety of different fun walls that are perfect for a unique photo. While walking down the streets of RiNo, you’ll definitely feel your creativity flowing.

    Riverfront Park

    • Built by an abandoned rail yard, Riverfront Park has a rare, urban vibe that is amazing for photography. There are many cool architectural points like the red accents and modern-shaped buildings. You’ll have to check this out for the perfect city pic!

    Denver Art Museum

    • If you haven’t been to the Denver Art Museum, you NEED to go. And be sure to wear a photo-worthy outfit, because this is the place to go for your next photo-op. You will want to stop at every painting here, the art is incredible and great for spicing up your Instagram feed.

    Pedestrian Alley at Dairy Block

    • If you want to enjoy dinner and ice cream while getting amazing photo content, you should plan a night at the Dairy Block. There is a pedestrian alley with string lights, art murals, and aesthetic outdoor furniture. This is perfect for a nighttime photoshoot. Who doesn’t love string lights, right?
